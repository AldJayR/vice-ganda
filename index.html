About Vice Ganda

Vice Ganda, born Jose Marie Borja Viceral, is a popular Filipino comedian, television host, actress, and recording artist. Known for her sharp wit, comedic timing, and iconic hosting style, she has become one of the most recognizable personalities in Philippine entertainment.

Career Highlights

  • Award-winning comedian and host of "It's Showtime"
  • Successful film actress with multiple box office hits
  • Influential media personality with massive social media following
  • Stand-up comedy performer and YouTube content creator
  • LGBTQ+ icon and advocate for diversity and inclusion
